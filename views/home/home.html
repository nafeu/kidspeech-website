Non-Medical Legal Independent Evaluation (IE)
Certified Life Care Plans

With more than three decades of industry experience, we know how important reliability and credibility are for the insurance industry.

We provide Speech Pathology Independent Examinations (IE), Paper File Reviews (PFR), Expert Opinions, and certified Life Care Plans (LCP). Our work focuses on cognitive communication skills in children and adults. Our comprehensive reports are of high quality and rely on both qualitative and quantitative data.

The modalities addressed in our reports are: Expressive and Receptive Language skills, Articulation and Phonology, Fluency, Voice, Oral Peripheral Examination, Literacy and Early Literacy skills, Reading and Writing skills, Swallowing, Digital Literacy, and Cognitive Communication (social, pragmatics, executive functions, and memory skills) to name a few areas.

Our Independent Examinations (IE) can, in some cases, be provided virtually so there is no transportation cost to the referral source. In other instances, when virtual is not an option, we provide services by following strict COVID-19 safety precautions.

We accept referrals for clients (babies, children, teens, adults or seniors) who experience difficulties after a concussion, an acquired brain injury, or a spinal cord injury which could have occurred in a car accident, road accident such as while riding a bike, motor bike, ski-doo, diving/swimming, at birth (negligence), during slips and falls, or in any other circumstances at home, school or in the community (sports).

Our Life Care Plans are often also requested by the legal community for the purpose of setting up a trust for minors or in other circumstances for an adult with a disability.
