Maria Scaringi

KIDSPEECH™ has been serving both children and adults since 1988. The founder, Maria Scaringi, is a registered Speech-Language Pathologist and a certified Canadian Life Care Planner who has worked with thousands of clients over the past three decades.

Originally from Montreal, Maria studied at McGill University and Clarion University of Pennsylvania where she was the recipient of several scholarships. She has lived and worked in Toronto, since 1985 and founded KIDSPEECH™ in 1988 as a boutique private clinic for clients of all ages with communication difficulties and associated injuries as a result of an acquired brain injury or concussion. Over the years numerous consultants have joined forces with Maria enabling KIDSPEECH™ to provide a wide range additional services beneficial to both children and adults

Maria has mentored many speech pathology graduate students in universities in both Canada, USA and UK, as well as communication disorder assistants. As a certified Canadian life Care Planner, she provides expert reports and detailed medical-legal strategic and life-long plans for children and adults who have sustained injuries to one or more organs. She provides Speech-Language Pathology Independent Examinations and expert reports for Insurance Companies and legal firms for clients who sustained injuries as a result of an accident

Maria’s other professional activities have included serving on various committees provincially, nationally, and internationally.

Maria's early clientèle in the late 80s believed in the services that KIDSPEECH™ provided and referred others who then in return referred additional satisfied clients who accessed screenings and assessments. This was how KIDSPEECH™ was founded 30 years ago
