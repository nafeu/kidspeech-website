Maria Scaringi

KIDSPEECH™ has been serving both children and adults since 1988. The founder, Maria Scaringi, is a registered Speech-Language Pathologist and a certified Canadian Life Care Planner who has worked with thousands of clients over the past three decades.

Originally from Montreal, Maria studied at McGill University and Clarion University of Pennsylvania where she was the recipient of several scholarships. She has lived and worked in Toronto, since 1985 and founded KIDSPEECH™ in 1988 as a boutique private clinic for clients of all ages with communication difficulties and associated injuries. Over the years numerous consultants have joined forces with Maria enabling KIDSPEECH™ to provide a wide range of additional services beneficial to both children and adults.

Our early clientèle in the late 80s believed in the services that KIDSPEECH™ provided and referred others who then in return referred additional satisfied clients who accessed screenings and assessments. This was how KIDSPEECH™ was founded some 30 years ago.
