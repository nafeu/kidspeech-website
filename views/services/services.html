Life Care Planning
Our certified Canadian Life-Care Plans are available to both defendant and plaintiff legal firms and insurance companies for both children and adults across Canada. We handle both catastrophic and non-catastrophic cases and offer a 20-minute free consultation before accepting a referral. Our plans are also available in cases wherein funds need to be appropriated over a lifetime for a child or adult with special needs. Our expertise is to deliver both testimony and opinions for legal firms.
Rehabilitation
Rehabilitation services are for both children and adults at home, in the community, school and at work. Programs are designed for each individual based on their needs focused on both academics, vocational and avocational skills. We provide case management services and work with a variety of health professionals to support the individual.
